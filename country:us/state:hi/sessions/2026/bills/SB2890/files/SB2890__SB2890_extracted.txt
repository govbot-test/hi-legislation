Section 1:
SB2890 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2890 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE STATE BUDGET. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Part V of Act 250, Session Laws of Hawaii 2025, is amended by amending section 14, item B-19, to read as follows: "19.      HAWAII FOODBANK, INC. PLANS, DESIGN, AND CONSTRUCTION[, AND EQUIPMENT] FOR [SOLAR PANEL INSTALLATION.] A NEW FACILITY.  THIS PROJECT QUALIFIES AS A GRANT PURSUANT TO CHAPTER 42F, HRS. TOTAL FUNDING          LBR            800 C                 C" SECTION 2. Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: State Budget; Grant; Chapter 42F; Hawaii Foodbank, Inc. Description: Amends the program scope of the grant authorized for Hawaii Foodbank, Inc. by Act 250, SLH 2025, pursuant to chapter 42F, HRS. Authorizes the grant moneys to be used for plans, design, and construction for a new facility.
