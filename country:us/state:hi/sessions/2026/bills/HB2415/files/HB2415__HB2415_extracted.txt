Section 1:
HB2415 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2415 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO traffic safety. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $          or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 to install all-way crosswalks at sites abutting school properties. The sum appropriated shall be expended by the department of transportation for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2026. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: DOT; Safe Routes to School; All-Way Crosswalks; Potential Sites; Appropriation Description: Appropriates moneys to the Department of Transportation to install all-way crosswalks at sites abutting school properties.
