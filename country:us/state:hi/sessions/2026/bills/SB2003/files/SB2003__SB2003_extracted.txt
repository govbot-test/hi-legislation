Section 1:
SB2003 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2003 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO NATURAL RESOURCE MANAGEMENT. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Act 255, Session Laws of Hawaii 2022, is amended as follows: 1.  By amending section 6 to read: "SECTION 6.  The auditor shall conduct a performance and financial audit of the Mauna Kea stewardship and oversight authority and shall submit a report on findings and recommendations to the legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the regular session of 2031.  The report shall include a recommendation as to whether the Mauna Kea stewardship and oversight authority is fit to continue management of Mauna Kea lands." 2.  By amending section 18 to read: "SECTION 18.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2022; provided that [part]: (1)  Part IV shall take effect on July 1, 2028[.]; and (2)  If the auditor recommends, in the report required under section 6 of this Act, that the Mauna Kea stewardship and oversight authority is unfit to continue management of Mauna Kea lands, then on December 31, 2031: (A)  Parts II, III, and IV of this Act shall be repealed and sections 28-8.3, 76-16, and chapter 304A, part IV, subpart O, Hawaii Revised Statutes, shall be reenacted in the form in which it read on the day before the effective date of this Act; provided further that the amendments made to section 76-16, Hawaii Revised Statutes, by: (i)  Section 2 of Act 42, Session Laws of Hawaii 2023; (ii)  Section 2 of Act 186, Session Laws of Hawaii 2023; (iii)  Section 2 of Act 66, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024; (iv)  Section 1 of Act 79, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024; (v)  Section 2 of Act 131, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024; (vi)  Section 2 of Act 165, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024; (vii)  Section 3 of Act 209, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024; (viii)  Section 2 of Act 247, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024; (ix)  Section 1 of Act 57, Session Laws of Hawaii 2025; (x)  Section 1 of Act 132, Session Laws of Hawaii 2025; and (xi)  Section 5 of Act 302, Session Laws of Hawaii 2025, shall not be repealed when section 76-16, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is reenacted on December 31, 2031, pursuant to this subparagraph; and (B)  Management authority over Mauna Kea lands, as defined in section 304A-1901, Hawaii Revised Statutes, shall revert to the university of Hawaii." SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Mauna Kea Lands; UH; MKSOA; Auditor; Act 255, SLH 2022 Description: Requires the Auditor's report pursuant to section 4 of Act 255, SLH 2022, to include a recommendation as to whether the Mauna Kea Stewardship and Oversight Authority is fit to continue management of Mauna Kea lands.  If the Auditor recommends that the Mauna Kea Stewardship and Oversight Authority is unfit to continue management of Mauna Kea lands, provides for the repeal of the Authority and reversion of management authority over Mauna Kea lands to the University of Hawaii.
