Section 1:
HB2399 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2399 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO EARLY LEARNING. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The legislature finds that prekindergarten-only public charter schools are an integral component to increasing access to early learning and meeting the mandates of Act 46, Session Laws of Hawaii 2020. Prekindergarten-only public charter schools meet the same rigorous requirements as public pre-kindergarten schools with the added benefit of being able to operate on a full-day schedule that better serves the needs of working families.  Prekindergarten-only public charter schools are essential to a high-quality mixed-delivery system, but are currently operating at a deficit. The purpose of this Act is to appropriate funds to the state public charter school commission to increase funding to prekindergarten-only public charter schools. SECTION 2.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for prekindergarten-only public charter schools. The sum appropriated shall be expended by the state public charter school commission for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: State Public Charter School Commission; Prekindergarten-only Public Charter Schools; Funding; Appropriation Description: Appropriates funds to the State Public Charter School Commission for prekindergarten-only public charter schools.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
