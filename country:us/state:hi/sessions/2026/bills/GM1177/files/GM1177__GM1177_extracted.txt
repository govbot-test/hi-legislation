A BILL FORAN ACT
RELATING TO NEIGHBORHOOD BOARDS.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. Section 92-81, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
2 amended by amending subsection (d) to read as follows:
3 "(d) A quorum for a meeting of a neighborhood board or
4 community outreach board shall be required for:
5 (1) Conducting official board business;
6 (2) Discussions before and related to voting; and
7 (3) Voting required to validate an act of the board as
8 part of official board business[—]£
9 provided that, if a neighborhood board overseen by a
10 neighborhood commission of the city and county of Honolulu has a
11 vacancy, notwithstanding section 92-15, a majority of the filled
12 seats on the neighborhood board shall constitute a quorum for
13 the purposes of paragraphs (1) and (2).
14 A neighborhood board or community outreach board may
15 receive information or testimony on a matter of official board
16 business without a quorum; provided that the board shall not
17 make a decision on the issue. The board members, at the next
