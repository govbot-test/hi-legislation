HB2341 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2341 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE UNIVERSITY OF HAWAII.
