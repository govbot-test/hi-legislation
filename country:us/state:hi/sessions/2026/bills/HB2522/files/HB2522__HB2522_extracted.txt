Section 1:
HB2522 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2522 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to taxation. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The purpose of this Act is to eliminate state income taxes. SECTION 2.  Chapter 235, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is repealed. SECTION 3.  No later than twenty days prior to the regular session of 2027, the department of taxation shall submit a report to the legislature on: (1)  The estimated annual tax revenue shortfall following the repeal of chapter 235, Hawaii Revised Statutes; (2)  Recommendations to address the expected revenue shortfall; and (3)  Proposed legislation to address the expected revenue shortfalls and for the necessary conforming amendments to the Hawaii Revised Statutes due to the repeal of chapter 235, Hawaii Revised Statutes. SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval; provided that section 2 shall apply to taxable years beginning after December 31, 2027. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Taxation; Income Tax; Repeal; DoTax Description: Eliminates the Hawaii state income tax starting with the 2028 taxable year.  Requires the Department of Taxation to submit a report to address the expected tax revenue shortfall and to propose conforming amendments to the Hawaii Revised Statutes.
