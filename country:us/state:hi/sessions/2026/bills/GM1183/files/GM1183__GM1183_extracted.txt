A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO EDUCATION.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that existing law lists
2 program areas that no longer align with federal requirements
3 under the Adult Education and Family Literacy Act of 1998,
4 reauthorized as title II of the Workforce Innovation and
5 Opportunity Act of 2014, and its implementing regulations in
6 title 34 Code of Federal Regulations part 463.
7 Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to update Hawaii's
8 statutory framework for adult and community education programs
9 to ensure alignment with federal adult education funding
10 requirements and current best practices in those programs by:
11 (1) Modernizing terminology to align with current adult
12 education practices;
13 (2) Adding English language acquisition and integrated
14 English literacy and civics education;
15 (3) Updating the term "homemaking and parent education" to
16 "family and community education";
