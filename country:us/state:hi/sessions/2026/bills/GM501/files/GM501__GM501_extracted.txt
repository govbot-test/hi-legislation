FROM: Josh Green, M.D.
Governor, State of Hawaii
SUBJECT: House District 36 Appointment
The Honorable Nadine K. Nakamura
Speaker
State House of Representatives
Hawai'i State Capitol, Room 431
Honolulu, HI 96813
Aloha Speaker Nakamura:
I am pleased to inform you that pursuant to section 17-4(a), Hawai'i Revised Statutes, I
have appointed Ms. Daisy Hartsfield to serve as the representative from House District
36 (Waipahu). Ms. Hartfield shall begin her service immediately, with a term expiring in
November 2026.
This fills the vacancy created by the December appointment of Representative Rachele
Lamosao to the Senate District 19 seat.
Mahalo,
sh Green, M.D.
overnor, State of Hawaii
