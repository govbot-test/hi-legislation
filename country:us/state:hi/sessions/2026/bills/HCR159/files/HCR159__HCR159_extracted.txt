HCR159 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.C.R. NO. 159 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION URGing the city and county of honolulu to take necessary steps to finalize, prioritize, and materialize the makakilo drive extension project. WHEREAS, the continued population growth in the Makakilo and greater Kapolei area has led to increased traffic congestion along existing access routes, particularly along Makakilo Drive and Farrington Highway; and WHEREAS, a 2009 environmental impact statement regarding potential avenues to relieve traffic congestion found that an extension proposed as part of greater planning for the Makakilo and Kapolei areas would not have significant adverse environmental impacts to native flora and fauna, existing land use, historical or cultural resources, and public services and facilities; and WHEREAS, while there has been long-standing advocacy from Makakilo residents and community members who have, for many years, requested that the extension project be completed, and despite various legislation proposed over the course of the last decade, no extension project to address the increased traffic congestion has materialized; and WHEREAS, it is a priority of the State to improve the regional connectivity between Makakilo, Kapolei, and the surrounding West Oahu communities; now, therefore, BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, the Senate concurring, that the City and County of Honolulu, including the Department of Transportation Services and other appropriate agencies, is requested to take necessary steps to finalize, prioritize, and materialize the Makakilo Drive Extension Project; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the City and County of Honolulu is encouraged to establish a clear timeline for the project that includes planning updates, funding identification, design, and construction phases; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the City and County of Honolulu is requested to submit a report, including a status update and implementation timeline for the project, to the Legislature no later than twenty days prior to the convening of the Regular Session of 2027; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that certified copies of this Concurrent Resolution be transmitted to the Mayor of the City and County of Honolulu, Director of the Department of Transportation Services of the City and County of Honolulu, and Chair of the Honolulu City Council. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: City and County of Honolulu; Makakilo Drive Extension Project
