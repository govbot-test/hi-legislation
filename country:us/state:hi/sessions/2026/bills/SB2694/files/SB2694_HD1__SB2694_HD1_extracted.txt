Section 1:
SB2694 HD1 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2694 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 S.D. 2 STATE OF HAWAII H.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO WATER CARRIERS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 271G, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§271G-     Cross-subsidization; prohibition.  (a)  Notwithstanding any law to the contrary, the commission shall not allow, require, approve, establish, or implement any rule, order, decision, or other regulatory action that provides for or results in cross-subsidization involving a water carrier. (b)  For purposes of this section, "cross-subsidization" means an internal mechanism utilized by a water carrier where additional revenues earned from profitable routes are used to cover the costs of non-profitable services, routes, or commodity types." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: PUC; Cross-subsidization; Prohibition Description: Prohibits the Public Utilities Commission from allowing, requiring, approving, establishing, or implementing any rule, order, decision, or other regulatory action that provides for or results in cross-subsidization involving a water carrier.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
