Aloha President Kouchi, Speaker Nakamura, and Members of the Legislature:

ACT 086
JUN 4 2026
THE SENATE
S.B. NO.
THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026
STATE OF HAWAII
C.D. 1
A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO AMBULANCES.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. There is appropriated out of the general
2 revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $1,750,000 or so much
3 thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the
4 purchase of one advanced life support ambulance and related
S equipment to be based in the central Maui area of the county of
6 Maui, and to fund pay-related personnel costs for one state-
7 certified emergency medical technician and one state-certified
8 paramedic.
9 The sum appropriated shall be expended by the department of
10 health for the purposes of this Act.
11 SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
