Section 1:
SR118 THE SENATE S.R. NO. 118 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII SENATE RESOLUTION Requesting the agribusiness development corporation to complete the purchase of the fee simple interest in the halaula agricultural parcel identified as tax map key:  (3) 5-3-7:15. WHEREAS, despite north Kohala's fertile land and abundant natural resources, the region has been historically underserved, lacking the support required to realize its agricultural potential which further contributes to the State's struggles with agriculture production and food security forcing it to rely on imported goods; and WHEREAS, investment in this region would alleviate these challenges by revitalizing the agricultural industry, creating employment opportunities, and improving food self-sufficiency across the State; and WHEREAS, the Agribusiness Development Corporation is uniquely positioned to oversee the purchase, management, and responsible development of agricultural lands in this region; and WHEREAS, the Halaula Agricultural Parcel located in north Kohala offers significant potential for agricultural development, including sustainable agricultural practices, farming, livestock, and agroforestry; and WHEREAS, the Agribusiness Development Corporation purchasing the Halaula Agricultural Parcel will stimulate economic growth, support community initiatives, and revitalize the rural economy in north Kohala; now, therefore, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, that the Agribusiness Development Corporation is requested to complete the purchase of the fee simple interest in the Halaula Agricultural Parcel identified as tax map key:  (3) 5-3-7:15; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that a certified copy of this Resolution be transmitted to the Executive Director of the Agribusiness Development Corporation. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: ADC; North Kohala; Agriculture; Agricultural Development;
