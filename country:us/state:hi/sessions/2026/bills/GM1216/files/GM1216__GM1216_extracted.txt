A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION PROCUREMENT.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that Act 134, Session
2 Laws of Hawaii 2025, provides a temporary adjustment, until its
3 June 30, 2028, sunset date, to the department of education's
4 small purchase procurement capacity by exempting purchases of
5 local edible produce and packaged food products of less than
6 $250,000 from the electronic procurement system requirement and
7 instead requiring no less than three written quotes for those
8 purchases.
9 The legislature also finds that, as the largest department
10 in the State with two hundred ninety-six schools and forty-two
11 thousand salaried and part-time employees, the department of
12 education must procure voluminous amounts of goods and services
13 to run its operation.
14 The legislature further finds that the department of
15 education is mandated to provide at least thirty per cent
16 locally sourced foods to Hawaii's public schools by 2030.
17 Increasing the department's small purchase procurement capacity

4 The legislature notes, however, that to effectuate Act 134,

6 statutory language allow for small purchase procurement of more
7 than $100,000 for local edible produce and packaged food

9 The purpose of this Act is to effectuate Act 134, Session
10 Laws of Hawaii 2025, by:
11 (1) Exempting department of education purchases of local
12 edible produce and packaged food products less than
13 $200,000 from the electronic procurement system; and
14 (2) Prohibiting the artificial division and parceling of
15 such procurements to be considered as small purchases.
