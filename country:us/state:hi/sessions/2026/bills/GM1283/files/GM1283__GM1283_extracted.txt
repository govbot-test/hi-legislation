A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO INTERNATIONAL RELATIONS.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that the existing scope
2 of chapter 229, Hawaii Revised Statutes, needs to be broadened
3 because, at present, it focuses largely on sister-state or
4 province partnerships. The legislature further finds that
5 modern government-to-government work requires the ability to
6 enter into nonbinding memoranda of understanding with national
7 governments or ministries, regional bodies, and other
8 international partners. This will enable the State to advance
9 in key areas, including agriculture, biosecurity, climate
10 resilience, indigenous knowledge, education, renewable energy,
11 and economic development.
12 The legislature also finds that it is important for
13 international agreements between the State and other governments
14 to be transparent to the public and subject to public input.
15 Therefore, the purpose of this Act is to:
16 (1) Authorize the State to enter into nonbinding
17 international cooperative agreements with national
