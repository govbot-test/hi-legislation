Section 1:
SB2407 HD1 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2407 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 S.D. 2 STATE OF HAWAII H.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO AGRICULTURAL LAND. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  There is established within the agribusiness development corporation one full time-equivalent (1.0 FTE) Hawaii island property manager position. SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: ADC; Agricultural Development; Hawaii Island; Property Manager; Position Description: Establishes one full-time equivalent (1.0 FTE) Hawaii Island Property Manager position within the Agribusiness Development Corporation.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
