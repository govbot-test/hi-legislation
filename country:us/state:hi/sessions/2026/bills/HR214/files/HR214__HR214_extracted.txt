Section 1:
HR214 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.R. NO. 214 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION relating to the committee assignments of the House of representatives of the thirty-third legislature. BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, that M. COV RATCLIFFE, who has been duly appointed and seated as a member of the House of Representatives from the  Twenty-eighth Representative District to fill the vacancy created by the resignation of former Representative Daniel Holt, shall be added as a member of the Economic Development & Technology Committee, Finance Committee, and Tourism Committee; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that except as amended by this Resolution, all appointments and committee memberships of the House of Representatives as set forth in H.R. No. 1 and H.R. No. 13 adopted by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, shall remain in full force and effect. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Committee Assignments; House of Representatives
