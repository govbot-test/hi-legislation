Section 1:
SB2789 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2789 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT Relating to capital improvement projects for the benefit of the sixteenth senatorial district. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The director of finance is authorized to issue general obligation bonds in the sum of $1,950,000 or so much thereof as may be necessary and the same sum or so much thereof as may be necessary is appropriated for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the purpose of capital improvement projects for the sixteenth senatorial district, as follows: 1.   Aiea High School A.   Installation of a new drainage system around the main gymnasium's exterior, including site preparation and demolition, leveling and grading, installation, paving, and contingency costs. Construction                    $225,000 Total funding                      $225,000 B.   Replacement of perimeter fencing surrounding the football stadium along Ulune street and Kaamilo street, including vertical-bar security fencing materials, demolition and site preparation, installation, and secure gates for controlled access to the stadium. Construction                    $105,000 Equipment                       $120,000 Total funding                      $225,000 2.   Webling Elementary School A.   Construction of a new two-story administration building. Plans and design              $1,500,000 Total funding                    $1,500,000 SECTION 2.  The appropriation made for the capital improvement projects authorized by this Act shall not lapse at the end of the fiscal biennium for which the appropriation is made; provided that all moneys from the appropriation unencumbered as of June 30, 2028, shall lapse as of that date. SECTION 3.  The sums appropriated for the respective capital improvement projects set forth in section 1 shall be expended by the department of education for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
