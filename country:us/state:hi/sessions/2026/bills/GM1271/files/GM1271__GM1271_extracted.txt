C.D. 1
A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO FERAL CHICKENS.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. There is appropriated out of the general
2 revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $50,000 or so much
3 thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 for the
4 city and county of Honolulu to facilitate the receipt, handling,
5 and humane disposition of feral chickens that have already been
6 caught.
7 The sum appropriated shall be expended by the city and
8 county of Honolulu for the purposes of this Act.
9 SECTION 2. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
