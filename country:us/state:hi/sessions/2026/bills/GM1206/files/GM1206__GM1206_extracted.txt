9 requirements that are only imposed in the county of Maui are the
10 requirements to submit the special license application in person
11 and provide a handwritten signature on the application. The
12 applicant must also obtain various separate departmental
13 clearances and produce detailed illustrative materials that are
14 duplicative of existing enforcement mechanisms. In addition, if
IS the applicant holds the fundraising event at a location
