Aloha President Kouchi, Speaker Nakamura and Members of the Legislature:
Transmitted herewith are proposed changes to the FY 27 Executive Supplemental
Budget for the operating and capital improvement program (CIP) budget requests, as
applicable, for the Departments of Agriculture and Biosecurity (AGR) and Land and
Natural Resources (LNR).
Your favorable consideration would be appreciated for the following budget requests to
amend the FY 27 Executive Supplemental Budget:

Section 2:
1. Adjustments to the Operating Budget Request:
The following budget requests are being proposed to update the amounts requested
in the operating budget:
Department of Land and Natural Resources
Parks Administration and Operations - Park Interpretation (LNR 806/FI)
Add $3,000,000 in special funds in FY 27 to increase the expenditure ceiling for land
management activities that include hazardous material remediation, debris removal,
tree trimming, and enforcement.
- 2 -

Section 3:
2. Adjustments to GIP Requests:
The following budget request is being proposed to update the amounts requested in
the GIP budget:
Department of Agriculture and Biosecurity
Agricultural Resource Management (AGR 141)
Add $28,800,000 in general obligation (G.O.) bond funds for FY 27 for plans, land
acquisition, design, construction, and equipment for improvements to the Wahiawa
Dam. The FY 27 Supplemental Executive Budget currently includes $15,000,000 in
G.O. bond funds for FY 27, so with this addition of $28,800,000, the entire Wahiawa
Dam GIP request for FY 27 will total $43,800,000.
Department of Land and Natural Resources
Parks Administration and Operations (LNR 806)
Add $4,000,000 in special funds in FY 27 for plans, design, and construction to
conduct additional testing for contamination and environmental hazards; to
implement remediation measures and debris clearing to protect public health and
safety; to ensure compliance with Department of Health requirements; and to
perform related work at Lake Wilson.
Thank you for your tireless work on behalf of the State of Hawai'i. Your favorable
consideration of these budget requests is greatly appreciated.
Mahala,
~~
Governor, State of Hawai'i
