Section 1:
SR2 THE SENATE S.R. NO. 2 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII SENATE RESOLUTION amending RULE 16 OF the rules of the senate of the thirty-third legislature, regular session of 2026, of the state of hawaii. BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, that the Rules of the Senate of the State of Hawaii be amended by amending paragraph 12 of Senate Rule 16 Standing Committees to read as follows: "(12)  Committee on Public Safety and Military Affairs (PSM).  The purview of this committee includes those programs relating to law enforcement, including but not limited to the narcotics division, the sheriffs division; county and state law enforcement officers; public safety; corrections and rehabilitation; civil defense; disaster readiness; state building code; [county and federal relations;] and military and veterans' affairs."; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Senate Rule material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the amendments made to the Senate Rules by this Resolution take effect upon the adoption of this Resolution. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ _____________________________ Report Title: Senate Rule 16; Standing Committees; Paragraph (12) Amendment; Adoption
