BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. Section 11-1.6, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
2 amended by amending subsection (a) to read as follows:
3 "(a) The chief election officer shall be appointed by the
4 elections commission, without regard to chapter 76. The
5 appointment shall not be subject to the advice and consent of
6 the senate. In the event of a vacancy [■;—fche]^
7 (1) The elections commission shall meet expeditiously to
8 select and appoint a new chief election officer to
9 serve the remainder of the unexpired term[-r]; and
10 (2) Pending the formal selection and appointment of the
11 new chief election officer, the following positions
12 within the office of elections shall temporarily act
13 in the position of chief election officer in the
14 following order of hierarchy:
15 (A) The supervisor of election support services to
16 the extent available to serve, and if not, then;
