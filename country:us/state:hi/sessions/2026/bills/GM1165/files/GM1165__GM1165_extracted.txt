regents pursuant to section [-{-] 304A-402 [+] ; and
8

10 degree on any campus of the university of
11 Hawaii."
12 SECTION 2. Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed
13 and stricken. New statutory material is underscored.
14 SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2026.
