Section 1:
HB2189 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2189 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to department of education funding. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 302A, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part V, subpart B, to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§302A-     Direct-to-school funding transparency.  No later than twenty days prior to the convening of each regular session, the department shall submit to the governor, board, and legislature, a report of: (1)  The amounts and sources of any moneys allocated to department schools in the prior year, aggregated by county; and (2)  The amounts allocated in the prior year to department administration." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: DOE; School Funding; Transparency; Reports Description: Requires the Department of Education to submit annual reports to the Legislature, Governor, and Board of Education disclosing the moneys the Department allocated in the prior year to department schools and department administration.
