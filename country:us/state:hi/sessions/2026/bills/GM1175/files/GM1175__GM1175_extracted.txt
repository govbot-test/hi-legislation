STATE OF HAWAII
A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO HARBOR SAFETY.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that Act 236, Session
2 Laws of Hawaii 2024 (Act 236), established requirements for
3 mooring operations to increase harbor safety and protect the
4 physical integrity of the State's commercial harbor system. The
5 legislature further finds that the requirements established by
6 Act 236 are subject to a sunset provision and are scheduled to
7 be repealed on July 1, 2028.
8 Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to ensure long-term
9 operational certainty and harbor security by repealing the
10 sunset provisions of Act 236.
11 SECTION 2. Act 236, Session Laws of Hawaii 2024, is
12 amended by amending section 5 to read as follows:
13 "SECTION 5. This Act shall take effect upon its approval [-f-
14 provided that t-hio Act shall be ropcalod on July 1,—"
15 SECTION 3. Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed
16 and stricken.
