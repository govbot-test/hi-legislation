Section 1:
SB3090 SD1 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 3090 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 S.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT MAKING EMERGENCY APPROPRIATIONS FOR PUBLIC EMPLOYMENT COST ITEMS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: PART I SECTION 1. This Act is recommended by the governor for immediate passage in accordance with section 9 of article VII of the Constitution of the State of Hawaii. PART II SECTION 2. There are appropriated or authorized from the sources of funding indicated below to collective bargaining statewide (BUF 102) the following sum or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2025-2026 to fund collective bargaining cost items associated with grievances filed under agreements negotiated with the exclusive representative of collective bargaining units (1) and (10), and for their excluded counterparts.  The amounts are for a negotiated settlement for the department of education and arbitration awards for the university of Hawaii and other executive branch departments related to temporary hazard pay.  The arbitration award for the other executive branch departments does not include amounts for the supplemental award that is subject to litigation. These amounts are in addition to the amounts provided in Act 29, Session Laws of Hawaii 2025: FY 2025-2026 General funds                      -0- Special funds                      -0- Federal funds                      -0- Other federal funds                -0- Trust funds                        -0- Interdepartmental transfers        -0- Revolving funds                    -0- SECTION 3. Funds appropriated or authorized by this part shall be allotted by the director of finance to the appropriate state departments for expenditure in fiscal year 2025-2026 for the purposes of this part. PART III SECTION 4. Salary increases and cost adjustments provided in this Act for any officer or employee whose compensation is paid, in whole or in part, from federal, special, or other funds shall be paid wholly or proportionately, as the case may be, from the respective funds; provided that if the respective funds are unable to support such payments, appropriations from other means of financing may be used with the approval of the Governor. SECTION 5. Funds appropriated or authorized by this Act that are not expended or encumbered by June 30, 2026, shall lapse as of that date. SECTION 6. This Act shall take effect on January 1, 2077. Report Title: Public Employment Cost Items; BU-1 and BU-10; Emergency Appropriations Description: Makes emergency appropriations for collective bargaining cost items for the members of Bargaining Units (1) and (10) and their excluded counterparts to resolve issues related to temporary hazard pay for fiscal year 2025-2026.  Effective 1/1/2077.  (SD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
