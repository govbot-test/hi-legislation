Section 1:
HB2410 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2410 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE HAWAII TECHNOLOGY DEVELOPMENT CORPORATION. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The legislature finds that the Hawaii technology development corporation serves as a facilitative catalyst for technology and innovation in the State and is intended to help align the State with emerging technologies related to computer software, biotechnology, ocean science, energy, nanotechnology, artificial intelligence, sensor and communication systems, medical devices and testing, aerospace, and defense. Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to support economic diversification by appropriating funds to the Hawaii technology development corporation to support certain programs. SECTION 2.  There is appropriated out of the general revenues of the State of Hawaii the sum of $           or so much thereof as may be necessary for fiscal year 2026-2027 to support economic diversification by providing funding for Hawaii technology development corporation programs, to be allocated as follows: (1)  $ to the Hawaii small business innovation research program to provide matching grants to help small businesses that receive funding from the federal small business innovation research and small business technology transfer programs further develop new products to solve critical issues; (2)  $ to the manufacturing assistance program to help Hawaii-based manufacturers become more globally competitive; and (3)  $ to the accelerator and small business training programs to help develop businesses that are solving large-scale problems in the community using innovative, advanced manufacturing, and technology-based solutions. The sum appropriated shall be expended by the Hawaii technology development corporation for the purposes of this Act. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: Hawaii Technology Development Corporation; Economic Diversification; Appropriation Description: Appropriates funds to the Hawaii Technology Development Corporation to support certain programs.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
