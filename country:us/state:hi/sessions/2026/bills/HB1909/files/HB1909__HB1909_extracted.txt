Section 1:
HB1909 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 1909 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to the budget. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 37, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part IV to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§37-     Repair and maintenance budget.  (a)  As part of the budget and supplemental budget pursuant to sections 37-71 and 37-72, respectively, the governor shall include a recommended budget for deferred repair and maintenance and capital improvements on existing state-owned buildings and infrastructure. (b)  Funds authorized by the legislature for deferred repair and maintenance and capital improvements on existing state-owned buildings and infrastructure shall be exempt from sections 37-31 to 37-41." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval.
