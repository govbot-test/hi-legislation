Section 1:
HCR1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.C.R. NO. 1 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION relating to recess days for the regular session of 2026. BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, the Senate concurring, that pursuant to article III, section 10, of the Hawaii State Constitution, the dates of the mandatory recess for the Regular Session of 2026 are hereby determined to be the 26th and 27th days of February and the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th days of March, on which dates the Regular Session of 2026 shall be recessed; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that pursuant to article III, section 10, of the Hawaii State Constitution, the dates of the recesses other than the dates of the mandatory recess for the Regular Session of 2026 are hereby determined to be the 27th and 29th days of January, the 9th, 11th, and 27th days of March, the 13th and 15th days of April, and the 4th, 5th, and 7th days of May, on which dates the Regular Session of 2026 shall be recessed. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Recess Days; House of Representatives
