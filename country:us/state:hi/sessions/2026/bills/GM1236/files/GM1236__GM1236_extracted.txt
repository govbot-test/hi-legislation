A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO STATE CONSTRUCTION PROJECTS.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. (a) There is established within the office of
2 the comptroller a five-year office of the state construction
3 manager pilot program, which shall be headed by a full-time
4 state construction manager, to provide guidance and
5 recommendations on the design review of state construction
6 projects pursuant to this section. The state construction
7 manager shall be hired by the comptroller without regard to
8 chapter 76, Hawaii Revised Statutes, and report to the
9 comptroller.
10 (b) To be eligible to serve in the position of state
11 construction manager, an individual shall have held a valid
12 license under chapter 464, Hawaii Revised Statutes, for at least
13 five years immediately preceding the date of hire.
14 (c) The state construction manager shall not engage in the
15 private practice of professional construction management or
16 serve in a managing capacity in any private business or
17 enterprise.
