Dear President Kouchi and Members of the Senate:
In accordance with the provisions of Article VI, Section 3 of the Hawaii State
Constitution, I have the honor to submit to the Senate for advise and consent the
nomination of Daniel M. Gluck to serve as Associate Judge of the State of Hawai'i
Intermediate Court of Appeals for a term of ten years.
Sincerely,
Josh Green, M.D.
Governor, State of Hawaii
c: The Honorable Nadine K. Nakamura, Speaker, State House of Representatives
The Honorable Karl Rhoads, Chairperson, Senate Committee on Judiciary
The Honorable Sabrina S. McKenna, Acting Chief Justice, Hawai'i State Supreme Court
Brandon M. Kimura, Administrative Director of the Courts, Hawai'i State Judiciary
