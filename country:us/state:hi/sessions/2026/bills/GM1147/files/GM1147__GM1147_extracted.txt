A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO THE UNIFORM PROBATE CODE.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The legislature finds that guardianship and
2 conservatorship proceedings under chapter 560, Hawaii Revised
3 Statutes, involve matters of a sensitive and private nature for
4 individuals whose capacity or incapacity becomes the subject of
5 those court proceedings. A court's determination of a person's
6 ability to receive and evaluate information, or to make or
7 communicate decisions, to meet essential requirements for
8 physical health, safety, or self-care necessarily involves
9 judicial review of reports, information, evaluations, and
10 medical records filed in judicial proceedings that describe a
11 person's mental or physical condition, diagnosis, and prognosis,
12 among other items of a highly personal and intimate nature.
13 The legislature further finds that court records and
14 judicial proceedings are presumptively open to the public unless
15 a compelling reason exists to restrict public access.
16 Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to amend the procedure
17 for sealing documents filed in guardianship and conservatorship
