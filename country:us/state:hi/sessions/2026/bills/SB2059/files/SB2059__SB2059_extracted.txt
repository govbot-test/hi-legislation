Section 1:
SB2059 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2059 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to law enforcement. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 139, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§139-    Immigration enforcement; delegation; prohibited.  No law enforcement agency shall enter into an agreement for the delegation of immigration enforcement authority to any state or county agency pursuant to title 8 United States Code Section 1357." SECTION 2. New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Federal Immigration Enforcement; Delegation; Prohibited Description: Prohibits a law enforcement agency from entering into an agreement for the delegation of immigration enforcement authority to a state or county agency.
