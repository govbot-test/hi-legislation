Section 1:
SB2866 CD1 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 2866 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 S.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII H.D. 1 C.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO KUPUNA HOUSING. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  The legislature finds that the state rent supplement program for kupuna administered by the Hawaii public housing authority has helped prevent some of the State's vulnerable elderly residents from facing eviction and becoming homeless. In total, the program has provided about three hundred at-risk kupuna with monthly rental assistance of less than $500 per month.  In 2024, the legislature funded the program with $1,000,000 and in 2025, the legislature extended the program's sunset date from 2026 to 2028.  The legislature believes that the program should now be made permanent. Accordingly, the purpose of this Act is to make the state rent supplement program for kupuna permanent. SECTION 2.  Act 98, Session Laws of Hawaii 2023, as amended by Act 282, Session Laws of Hawaii 2025, is amended by amending section 6 to read as follows: "SECTION 6.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2023[, and shall be repealed on June 30, 2028]." SECTION 3.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken. SECTION 4.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 2026. Report Title: HPHA; State Rent Supplement Program for Kupuna Description: Repeals the sunset date for the State Rent Supplement Program for Kupuna.  (CD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
