Section 1:
HB2335 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 2335 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 76, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to part I to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§76-     Interns; eligible for internal recruitment.  Notwithstanding any other law to the contrary, an intern who has completed at least four hundred eighty hours of work experience acquired through a state-funded internship or workforce development program within a department or agency of the State shall be deemed eligible for internal recruitment within that department or agency." SECTION 2. New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: Workforce Development; Internships; Internal Recruitment Description: Establishes that certain participants in state-funded internship and workforce development programs are eligible for internal recruitment.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
