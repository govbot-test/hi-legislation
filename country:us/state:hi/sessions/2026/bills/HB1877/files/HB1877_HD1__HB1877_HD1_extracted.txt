Section 1:
HB1877 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 1877 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO THE HAWAII STATE LESBIAN, GAY, BISEXUAL, TRANSGENDER, QUEER, PLUS COMMISSION. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Section 369-3, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended as follows: 1. By amending subsection (b) to read: "(b)  The commission shall consist of the following: (1)  Six ex officio, nonvoting members or their designees, including the: (A)  Superintendent of education; (B)  President of the University of Hawaii system; (C)  Director of labor and industrial relations; (D)  Director of human resources development; (E)  Director of human services; and (F)  Director of health; and (2)  [Eight] Nine voting members who shall be appointed by the governor in accordance with section 26-34; provided that of these [eight] nine members: (A)  One member shall be from the county of Hawaii; (B)  One member shall be from the county of Kauai; (C)  One member shall be from the county of Maui; (D)  Four members shall be from the city and county of Honolulu; [and] (E)  One member shall represent the Hawaii sexual and gender minority workgroup of the department of health[.]; and (F)  One member shall be under the age of twenty-nine at the time of the member's appointment; and (3)  Two voting members, one of whom shall be appointed by the speaker of the house of representatives and one of whom shall be appointed by the senate president." 2. By amending subsection (d) to read: "(d)  A chairperson of the commission shall be elected annually from the [eight] eleven appointed members of the commission.  A quorum to do business or validate any act of the commission shall consist of a majority of the [eight] eleven appointed members of the commission." SECTION 2.  Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed and stricken.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect on July 1, 3000. Report Title: Hawaii State Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer Plus Commission; Membership Description: Expands the membership of the Hawaii State Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer Plus Commission from eight voting members to eleven by adding one seat for a person under the age of twenty-nine and one seat each for the appointees of the Speaker of the House of Representatives and Senate President, respectively.  Effective 7/1/3000.  (HD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
