Section 1:
SB3055 SD2 THE SENATE S.B. NO. 3055 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 S.D. 2 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO FALSE IMPERSONATION OF UNION REPRESENTATIVES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 89, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§89-    Employee organization representative; false impersonation; prohibited; cause of action; penalties.  (a) No person shall falsely impersonate an employee organization representative. (b)  An employee organization alleging a violation of this section may bring a civil action in a court of competent jurisdiction for injunctive relief; payment of damages, which may include punitive damages; or other appropriate relief. (c)  For the purposes of this section: "Employee organization representative" means: (1)  An employee, staff member, or any agent of an employee organization; (2)  A member of an employee organization who serves in an elected or appointed position within the employee organization; or (3)  A public employee who is designated by the exclusive representative as a representative for the employees in an appropriate bargaining unit. "Falsely impersonate an employee organization representative" means to use fraud, misrepresentation, material omission with the specific intent to deceive, or deceit in making a verbal or written communication that purports to be authorized or otherwise approved by an employee organization but has not been authorized or approved by the employee organization." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Report Title: Public Employees; Employee Organization Representative; False Impersonation; Prohibition; Cause of Action; Penalties Description: Prohibits a person from falsely impersonating an employee organization representative.  Establishes a cause of action and penalties for violations.  (SD2) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
