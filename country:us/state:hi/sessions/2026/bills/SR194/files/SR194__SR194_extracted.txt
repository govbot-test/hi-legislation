SR194 THE SENATE S.R. NO. 194 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII SENATE RESOLUTION RETURNING ALL BILLS, CONCURRENT RESOLUTIONS, AND RESOLUTIONS TO THE CLERK'S DESK.
