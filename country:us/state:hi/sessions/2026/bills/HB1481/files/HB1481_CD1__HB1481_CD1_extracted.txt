Section 1:
HB1481 CD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 1481 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2025 H.D. 2 STATE OF HAWAII S.D. 1 C.D. 1 A BILL FOR AN ACT RELATING TO HUMAN REMAINS. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 531B, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§531B-     Disposition of remains.  (a)  Any cemetery, crematory, funeral establishment, hydrolysis facility, or mortuary shall dispose of the remains of a dead human body no later than sixty days from the later of either the date a permit has been issued pursuant to section 338-23 or the date of an affidavit for amendment of the permit has been submitted.  The department of health shall not be required to include the specific disposition date on the permit. (b)  The department of health may adopt rules pursuant to chapter 91 necessary for the purposes of this section." SECTION 2. New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3. This Act shall take effect upon its approval. Report Title: DOH; Disposition of Remains; Burial-transit Permit; Cemetery; Crematory; Funeral Establishment; Hydrolysis Facility; Mortuary Description: Requires any cemetery, crematory, funeral establishment, hydrolysis facility, or mortuary to dispose of the remains of a dead human body no later than sixty days from the later of either the date a burial‑transit permit has been issued or the date of an affidavit for amendment of the permit has been submitted.  (CD1) The summary description of legislation appearing on this page is for informational purposes only and is not legislation or evidence of legislative intent.
