Section 1:
HB1637 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.B. NO. 1637 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII A BILL FOR AN ACT relating to nonprofits. BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII: SECTION 1.  Chapter 414D, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is amended by adding a new section to be appropriately designated and to read as follows: "§414D-     Attorney general; authority to investigate.  The attorney general may, on its own initiative or at the request of the department of taxation, investigate a corporation to ensure the corporation is in compliance with the provisions of this chapter and those of section 501(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986, as amended." SECTION 2.  New statutory material is underscored. SECTION 3.  This Act shall take effect upon its approval. INTRODUCED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Nonprofit Corporations; Attorney General; Investigation Description: Authorizes the Department of the Attorney General to initiate an investigation into a nonprofit corporation for compliance with the Hawaii Nonprofit Corporations Act and its tax exempt status under the Internal Revenue Code.
