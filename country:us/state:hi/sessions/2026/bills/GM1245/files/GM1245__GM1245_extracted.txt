A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO CAFETERIA PLANS.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. Section 78-30, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
2 amended by amending subsection (a) to read as follows:
3 "(a) Each chief executive may establish a wage and salary
4 reduction benefit program [whieh] that qualifies as a cafeteria
5 plan within the meaning of section 125 of the Internal Revenue
6 Code of 1986, as amended[—]; provided that the maximum salary
7 reduction contribution and maximum carryover amount for any
8 flexible spending account plan established pursuant to this
9 section shall be set annually in accordance with the maximum
10 amounts allowed under section 125 of the Internal Revenue Code,
11 as it operates in the State under chapter 235. The cafeteria
12 plan shall allow eligible employees to elect to reduce their
13 pretax compensation in return for payment by the jurisdiction of
14 the expenses of eligible benefits."
15 SECTION 2. Statutory material to be repealed is bracketed
16 and stricken. New statutory material is underscored.
