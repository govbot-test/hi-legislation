HR73 HD1 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.R. NO. 73 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 H.D. 1 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION REQUESTING THE PLANNING OR PLANNING AND PERMITTING DEPARTMENT OF EACH COUNTY TO ESTABLISH KUPUNA-FRIENDLY BUILDING PERMIT REQUIREMENTS FOR PARKING ACCESSIBILITY AND PRIVATE BUSINESSES OPERATING HOURS. WHEREAS, parking is a foundational access issue for people with disabilities and older adults, with inadequate parking availability increasing safety risks, including falls and injuries; and WHEREAS, when accessible and van accessible parking spaces are insufficient, kupuna with mobility disabilities are effectively excluded from participating in community life, accessing services, and engaging in employment, commerce, and civic activities; and WHEREAS, according to the Disability and Communication Access Board, individuals who qualify for accessible parking spaces often cannot find available parking spaces that accommodate their needs; and WHEREAS, additionally, during the COVID-19 pandemic, many stores adjusted their hours for kupuna and customers with vulnerable immune systems, which successfully allowed greater access to goods and services for kupuna; and WHEREAS, it is critical for the State to increase opportunities for kupuna to participate in the community and access necessary goods and services; now, therefore, BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, that the planning or planning and permitting department of each county is requested to establish kupuna-friendly building permit requirements to plan for the State's aging population that: (1)  Require private businesses and malls to accommodate special hours for kupuna to enter; and (2)  Increase the minimum number of parking spaces designated for non-disabled and disabled kupuna; and BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that certified copies of this Resolution be transmitted to the Mayor of each county, members of each county council, Director of the Department of Planning and Permitting of the City and County of Honolulu, Planning Director of the Hawaii County Planning Department, Planning Director of the Maui County Planning Department, and Planning Director of the Kauai County Planning Department. Report Title: County Planning; Building Permits; Kupuna; Parking; Operating Hours
