HR209 HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES H.R. NO. 209 THIRTY-THIRD LEGISLATURE, 2026 STATE OF HAWAII HOUSE RESOLUTION COMMENDING TUMUA TUINEI FOR HIS OUTSTANDING ACCOMPLISHMENTS AS A LEADING FIGURE IN HAWAIʻIS ENTERTAINMENT COMMUNITY, HIS COLLABORATION WITH THE STATE OF HAWAIʻI TO SUPPORT YOUTH WELLNESS, AND HIS POSITIVE, FAR‑REACHING SOCIAL MEDIA PRESENCE THAT UPLIFTS AND PERPETUATES HAWAIʻIS CULTURE WHILE BRINGING LAUGHTER TO THE PEOPLE OF HAWAIʻI. WHEREAS, TUMUA TUINEI, born on January 21, 1996, in Honolulu, Oʻahu, grew up surrounded by the values of family, humility, and hard work that shaped his early life and future career, and from a young age demonstrated a natural ability to connect with others through humor, relatability, and a deep appreciation for Hawaiʻis local culture; and WHEREAS, TUMUA TUINEI, the son of Tom and Louise Tuinei of Honolulu, Oʻahu, graduated from Punahou School in the Class of 2014, and followed a path inspired by athletic excellence by enrolling at the University of Hawaiʻi at Mānoa, where he majored in Communicology with a minor in Theatre while excelling as a Division I defensive back for the University of Hawaiʻi Rainbow Warriors football team from 2014 to 2018, demonstrating perseverance, discipline, and leadership throughout his collegiate career; and WHEREAS, TUMUA TUINEI discovered his passion for comedy while attending UH Mānoa, taking a stand up class that led to his first performance at Anna OBriens comedy club; balancing the demands of being a student athlete, he continued to refine his craft at local venues throughout Honolulu, eventually headlining his first show before an audience of more than 300 attendees and steadily developing the comedic voice that would define his career, and later rose to prominence during the COVID 19 pandemic through his humorous, local style sketches on social media, creating content that resonated deeply with Hawaiʻi residents and the broader Polynesian community and expanding his reach across multiple platforms, including Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube, where he amassed more than 700,000 followers who connected with his authenticity, cultural insight, relatable humor, and vibrant personality; and WHEREAS, TUMUA TUINEI extended his impact beyond entertainment in 2021 by partnering with the Hawaiʻi Department of Health to support its TeenLink Hawaiʻi public awareness campaign, using his comedy and recognizable style to strengthen youth resources statewide, promote wellness and mental health, encourage positive communication within families, and make important information more accessible to teens and caregivers, and has since emerged as one of Hawaiʻis most prominent contemporary comedians, selling out major venues across the state, including 18 sold out shows at Blue Note Hawaiʻi in 2021 and multiple sold out nights at the Blaisdell Concert Hall, and expanding nationally and internationally through a 2024 tour that reached 24 cities, 14 states, 5 islands, and 2 countries, while releasing two stand up specials, Strictly Aloha and Talk Story: The Crowd Work Special, earning widespread acclaim and further solidifying his impact on modern Hawaiʻi entertainment; and WHEREAS, TUMUA TUINEI, supported by his wife, Rachael Ostovich, with whom he shares a profound commitment to family, culture, and community, and together they welcomed their daughter, Dovey Pearl Taliʻilani Tuinei, on May 15, 2024, strengthening their ʻohana and deepening their dedication to raising their child with aloha and pride in her Hawaiian heritage; now, therefore, BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives of the Thirty-third Legislature of the State of Hawaii, Regular Session of 2026, hereby commends TUMUA TUINEI for his achievements in comedy and entertainment, recognizes his rise from a young local athlete to a nationally touring comedian who has brought pride, laughter, and a renewed sense of cultural identity to audiences across Hawaiʻi and beyond, acknowledges his commitment to public service through impactful partnerships such as the TeenLink Hawaiʻi campaign, celebrates his role as a cultural ambassador whose work preserves and uplifts Hawaiʻis humor, language, and community spirit, and honors his dedication to family as a husband and father who carries forward the values of aloha, humility, and ʻohana, and extends its sincere appreciation for his admirable efforts to uplift Hawaiʻis culture, community, and spirit through humor, creativity, hard work, and a heartfelt commitment to the people of Hawaiʻi. OFFERED BY: _____________________________ Report Title: Tumua Tuinei; Performing Arts; Entertainment; Comedy
