C.D. 1
A BILL FOR AN ACT
RELATING TO HOME HEALTH LICENSING.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF HAWAII:
1 SECTION 1. The purpose of this Act is to authorize the
2 department of health to accept accreditation or certification
3 from an accreditation organization or a certification
4 organization approved by the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid
5 Services as demonstration that a licensed home health agency is
6 in compliance with all licensing inspections required by the
7 State.
8 SECTION 2. Section 321-91, Hawaii Revised Statutes, is
9 amended to read as follows:

14 health services shall mean ancillary paramedical services
15 rendered in the patient's home, and shall include [7-] but not be
16 limited to [7-] professional nursing care, physiotherapy,
17 occupational therapy, speech and hearing therapy, medical social
