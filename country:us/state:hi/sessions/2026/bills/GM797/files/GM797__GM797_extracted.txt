In accordance with the provisions of Article VI, Section 3 of the Hawaii’ State

of the Fifth Circuit (island of Kaua'i), State of Hawai'i, for a term of ten years.
Sincerely,
Josh Green, M.D.
Governor, State of Hawaii
c; The Honorable Michael K. Soong, District Court Judge, Sth Circuit
